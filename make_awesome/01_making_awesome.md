# Why Ruby? #
## System facilities
* <code>system("whatever you want")</code>
* <code>stdout = \`some other command\`</code>
* <code>$?</code> gets access to results, e.g. <code>$?.success?</code>
* <code>FileUtils</code> - cross platform UNIX-style
* More portable than <code>sh</code>
